Zamaane di nazran ton hide rakhan tenu,
Main dil de kone vich guide karaan tenu,
Sari duniya bhulake main focus karaan,
Tere hassan di khaatir main joker banaan,
Main marr mita haan teri har ik gal te,
Tera dhyan rakhda haan aane wale kal te,
Ni munde sare sadhde ne jealous hoke,
Main pyar nibhawanga tera true hoke.
