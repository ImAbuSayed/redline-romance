Agg wargi taseer teri hot lagdi,
Mere seene vich goli vangu shot vajdi,
Main engine di aawaz karaan bilkul mute,
Jadon paake tu aave oh kaala jeha suit,
Main aakad ni jarda par humble haan aaj,
Tu dil utte kardi ae raani wangu raaj,
Tera gussa vi lagda ae sweet mainu jaan,
Main tere utte vaarda haan sab armaan.
