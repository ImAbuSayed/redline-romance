Oh teri adavaan te main crazy hoya,
Raatan di neend chad ke main lazy hoya,
Main takda haan tenu right from the start,
Tu meri zindgi da ban gayi ae part,
Oh sohniye tu dil vich fire laa gayi,
Mere saahan de vich tu desire paa gayi,
Main tenu chahnda haan letting go of fear,
